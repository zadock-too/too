Food Recipe
Spaghetti Bolognese Cook spaghetti and top with a sauce made of minced beef, tomatoes, onions, and Italian herbs.
Caesar Salad Toss romaine lettuce with Caesar dressing, croutons, Parmesan cheese, and grilled chicken.
Margherita Pizza Top a pizza base with tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella, basil, and a drizzle of olive oil. Bake until golden.
Chocolate Cake Mix flour, cocoa powder, sugar, eggs, butter, and baking powder. Bake and top with chocolate frosting.
Grilled Salmon Marinate salmon in olive oil, lemon, and garlic. Grill until cooked through and serve with vegetables.
